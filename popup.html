Listening
Whoops, you denied Nat permission to listen for commands

To fix this:

  • Open Chrome's Settings
  • In the search bar in the top right corner, search 'microphone'
  • Click 'Content settings'
  • Scroll down to the Microphone section at the bottom and click "Manage Exceptions"
  • Find all lines starting with "chrome-extension://" and click the 'x' to remove them from the block list
  • Close and re-open Nat
  • Click 'Allow' after Nat re-opens
